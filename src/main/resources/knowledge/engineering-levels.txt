ENGINEERING LEVELS FRAMEWORK

L3 - Software Engineer I
Technical Scope: Implements well-defined tasks within a single component or feature area.
Expected Output: Completes assigned tickets with minimal supervision; produces working, tested code.
Collaboration: Works within a squad; relies on senior engineers for design direction.
Ownership: Owns individual tasks, not entire features.
Impact: Task-level impact within a team.

L4 - Software Engineer II
Technical Scope: Designs and implements features end-to-end within a service or domain.
Expected Output: Ships features independently; writes design docs for medium-complexity changes.
Collaboration: Coordinates with adjacent teams; unblocks self; mentors interns and L3s informally.
Ownership: Owns features from design to production; on-call for services they build.
Impact: Feature-level impact; improves team velocity.

L5 - Senior Software Engineer
Technical Scope: Leads technical direction for a sub-domain; cross-service design decisions.
Expected Output: Drives technical roadmap for a team; creates leverage through tooling, patterns, or documentation.
Collaboration: Actively mentors L3/L4 engineers; strong cross-functional partner; influences product direction.
Ownership: Owns an entire domain or service; accountable for reliability and quality metrics.
Impact: Team-level impact; decisions shape what the team builds and how.

L6 - Staff Engineer
Technical Scope: Cross-team technical strategy; sets standards that others follow.
Expected Output: Solves org-level technical problems; leads multi-team initiatives; drives architectural decisions.
Collaboration: Works across multiple teams; influences engineering culture; represents engineering in leadership forums.
Ownership: Owns technical strategy for a product area or platform; drives multi-quarter roadmaps.
Impact: Organization-level impact; outcomes affect multiple teams or products.

L7 - Principal Engineer
Technical Scope: Company-wide technical influence; identifies and solves high-leverage problems at scale.
Expected Output: Defines technical vision; shapes hiring and talent strategy; external thought leadership.
Collaboration: Partners with VPs and Directors; shapes company engineering culture.
Ownership: Owns technical strategy for a business unit or entire engineering org.
Impact: Company-level impact; work shapes engineering direction for years.

L8 - Distinguished Engineer / Fellow
Technical Scope: Industry-level influence; pioneer in domain.
Expected Output: Defines category-level thinking; patents, research, keynotes.
Collaboration: Advises C-suite; represents company externally.
Ownership: Owns technical legacy of the company.
Impact: Industry-level impact.
